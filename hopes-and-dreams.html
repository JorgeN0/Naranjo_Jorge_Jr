Looking Ahead

Career Aspirations

My ultimate career goal is to work at the forefront of Artificial Intelligence research and development. I aspire to contribute to projects that have a meaningful, positive impact on society — whether that means building smarter healthcare tools, improving accessibility through technology, or pushing the boundaries of what AI can achieve.

Academic Goals

After completing my Bachelor of Science at the University of South Carolina, I hope to go one to work in artificial technology's specifically something related to robotics or AI image screening to really push the boundaries of what AI can do.

Personal Dreams

Beyond my professional ambitions, I hope to grow as a person — to travel, experience new cultures, and continually broaden my perspective. I dream of giving back to my community by mentoring the next generation of students who, like me, are eager to break into the tech world.

Making a Difference

At the heart of all my hopes and dreams is a desire to make a difference. I want to build technology that is ethical, inclusive, and accessible to everyone. I believe that computer science and mathematics are powerful tools for solving the world's greatest challenges, and I am committed to using those tools responsibly and creatively throughout my career.
