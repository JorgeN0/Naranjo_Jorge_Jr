My Translation Work

Background

I have been providing professional Spanish translation services since 2024. As a fluent bilingual speaker of both English and Spanish, I offer accurate, culturally sensitive translations that meet the specific requirements of specialized industries. My work spans technical and professional documents, with a strong emphasis on precision and clarity.

Physical Therapy Translation

A significant portion of my translation work is within the physical therapy field. I have translated a variety of documents including:

  • Patient intake forms and medical history questionnaires
  • Exercise instruction sheets and home exercise programs
  • Post-operative rehabilitation protocols
  • Progress notes and clinical documentation
  • Patient education materials on injury prevention and recovery

These translations help healthcare providers communicate effectively with Spanish-speaking patients, ensuring they fully understand their treatment plans and exercises.

Legal Translation

I have also taken on various legal translation assignments, handling documents that require strict attention to terminology and formatting. My legal translation work includes:

  • Contracts and agreements
  • Affidavits and sworn statements
  • Court documents and legal correspondence
  • Immigration forms and supporting documentation
  • Notices and official communications

Accuracy and confidentiality are paramount in legal translation, and I approach every assignment with the care and professionalism these documents demand.
